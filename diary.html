Framing good times and happy minds in Germany
23rd December 2017
Ben
I liked Hong Li, first for her verbal delivery, then for her photographic prowess, and then for her writing after the book came out. "Covered with golden hair and snow white." Talking about the origins of her husband's Germanic people, Hong Li used these eight words to describe the sight of them coming to this world from the mountains. I was mesmerised by her poetic language. I love words, and Hong Li is partial to a person with great linguistic ability, and she always speaks in a particularly light and white language that is subdued and poetic.
